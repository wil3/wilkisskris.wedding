Kristen & Wil's Wedding

July 23rd 2016
Little Compton, Rhode Island

Welcome Party

For those arriving early, we welcome you to join us Friday July 22nd 2016 at the Stone House Inn Barn at 8pm to 10pm for the Welcome Party. Jam on!

Ceremony

The ceremony will be held on Rock Beach at 4:30pm, down the street from the Stone House Inn located at 122 Sakonnet Point Rd, Little Compton, RI 02837. Parking and shuttle drop off will be at Stone House and we will walk a short distance to the beach, wear comfy shoes . In the unfortunate event weather does not cooperate, a tented ceremony will take place on the Stone House lawn.

Reception

Following the ceremony, cocktail hour and reception will take place on the Stone House lawn. Bring your dancing shoes for an epic dance party!

After Party

Under the Stone House is a traditional speakeasy which we will continue to indulge in libations and rock out. Once things mellow out, we'll have an after, after party on the lawn of the Stone House with a bonfire, s'mores stuff and acoustic jams.

Accommodations

We have reserved rooms at two hotels, both of which we will provide shuttles to and from the Stone House. Call and reserve under Carter-Koch. Hampton Inn (508) 675-8500 and Comfort Inn (508) 672-0011. Additionally, there are many rentals on VRBO if you're looking to make a holiday of the wedding.

Things To Do

Surf or catch some rays at Second beach, Newport. Check out the yatchs, sailboats, and dozen of restaraunts and bars in downtown Newport. Explore the coast of Newport via the Cliff Walk.

Transportation

Shuttle service will be provided to and from the Hampton Inn and Comfort Inn. The bus schedule is as follows: Hotel Depart 3pm, Stone House Inn Depart 10pm , 11:30pm, 1pm.
